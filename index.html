1. Introduction (Vision)

NeuroMint represents the next evolution of Web3: decentralized AI power for everyone. Our goal is to make the potential of artificial intelligence more open, fair, and transparent through blockchain technology. We believe AI shouldn’t belong to tech giants – it should belong to the community.

2. Problem & Solution

Problem:
Artificial intelligence is currently centralized (Google, OpenAI, Meta). Control over AI lies in the hands of a few corporations. Web3 infrastructure is often too complex and inaccessible.

Solution:
NeuroMint provides a decentralized platform for AI tools. Users can use, build, and improve AI – powered by the NeuroMint Token (NMNT). Everything is open, transparent, and community-driven.
