NeuroMint – Mini Whitepaper

1. Introduction (Vision)

NeuroMint stands for the next evolutionary stage in Web3: decentralized AI power for everyone. Our goal is to make the capabilities of artificial intelligence accessible, fair, and transparent through blockchain technology.

2. Problem & Solution

Problem:
Artificial intelligence is currently highly centralized (Google, OpenAI, Meta). Control over AI lies in the hands of a few corporations. Web3 infrastructures are often complex and not accessible.

Solution:
NeuroMint offers a decentralized platform for AI tools. Users can use, create, and improve AI — powered by the NeuroMint Token (NMNT). Everything is open, transparent, and democratic.

3. Technology

  • Blockchain-based (Layer-2 solution, scalable)
  • AI services via decentralized networks
  • Focus on privacy-first AI and open source
  • Smart contracts regulate usage, payments, and further development

4. Tokenomics

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 NMNT

Distribution:

  • 50% Community airdrop and rewards
  • 20% Team (with 2 years vesting)
  • 20% Ecosystem development
  • 10% Reserve

Utility:

  • Access to AI tools
  • Staking for governance
  • Payments within the platform
